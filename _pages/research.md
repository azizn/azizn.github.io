## Research Clusters

<div style="margin-top: 30px;">

<h3 style="color: #667eea; margin-bottom: 15px;">5G/6G Wireless Communications & Signal Processing</h3>
<div style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #667eea 0%, #764ba2 100%); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; color: white; margin-bottom: 20px;">
<p style="margin-bottom: 15px;">This is my most active research area, focusing on next-generation wireless communication systems. Key topics include:</p>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
<li><b>PAPR Reduction</b>: Developing hybrid algorithms (PTS, SLM, genetic algorithms, neural networks) to reduce Peak-to-Average Power Ratio in OFDM, NOMA, OTFS, and FBMC waveforms</li>
<li><b>Signal Detection</b>: Deep learning-based signal detectors (LSTM, Bi-LSTM, DNN, CNN) for massive MIMO systems</li>
<li><b>Spectrum Sensing</b>: Cognitive radio techniques and energy detection methods for 5G/6G networks</li>
<li><b>NOMA/OTFS/MIMO Systems</b>: Non-orthogonal multiple access and orthogonal time frequency space modulation for beyond 5G</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p style="margin-bottom: 30px;"><b>Selected Publications:</b><br>
• Kumar, A., Gaur, N., & Nanthaamornphong, A. (2025). Enhanced PAPR reduction in NOMA systems using modified SLM and PTS techniques. <i>Open Engineering</i>. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1515/eng-2025-0153">(doi)</a><br>
• Kumar, A., Nanthaamornphong, A., Gaur, N., & Masud, M. (2025). Low complexity hybrid algorithm for improving PAPR, BER, and PSD in OTFS. <i>Scientific Reports</i>. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-025-26748-w">(doi)</a><br>
• Kumar, A., Nanthaamornphong, A., & Masud, M. (2025). RNN-Bi-LSTM spectrum sensing algorithm for NOMA waveform. <i>Scientific Reports</i>. <a href="https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-025-16414-6">(doi)</a></p>

<h3 style="color: #f5576c; margin-bottom: 15px;">Optical & Visible Light Communications</h3>
<div style="background: linear-gradient(135deg, #f093fb 0%, #f5576c 100%); padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; color: white; margin-bottom: 20px;">
<p style="margin-bottom: 15px;">Research on optical communication systems for high-speed data transmission:</p>
<ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
<li><b>Optical OFDM/NOMA</b>: Signal processing techniques for optical waveforms with high-order QAM (256-QAM, 512-QAM, 1024-QAM)</li>
<li><b>VLC Applications</b>: Visible light communication systems for indoor positioning and data transmission</li>
<li><b>Free Space Optical (FSO)</b>: Performance analysis under diverse channel conditions</li>
<li><b>Underwater Communications</b>: Optical network frameworks for underwater applications</li>
</ul>
</div>
